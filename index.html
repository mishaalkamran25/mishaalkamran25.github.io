Background

Finance roots.
Analytics edge.

I started my career in finance — working through financial statements, cost structures, and budget cycles at The Coca-Cola Company. That foundation gave me a sharp sense for what numbers actually mean inside a business.

Now, at Purdue's Daniels School of Business, I'm layering advanced analytics on top of that finance instinct. I work across Python, SQL, Power BI, and Azure AI — not to replace business judgment, but to make it faster and more defensible.

What I care about: building things that get used. Dashboards stakeholders actually open. Forecasts teams actually trust. Analysis that lands in the room where decisions are made.
